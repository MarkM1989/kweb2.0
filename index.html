What is Kivotos

Kivotos is the Greek word for Ark, which is in reference to the famous story of Noah’s Ark. Just like Noah stored the animals and used them to multiply the earth, we at kivotos.tech take your business and use it to multiply your digital buisness and take it to the next level.
